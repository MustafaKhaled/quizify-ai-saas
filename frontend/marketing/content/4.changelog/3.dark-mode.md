The first language-learning track is live: **German Grammar A1**, calibrated to Goethe-Zertifikat A1 / telc A1 / ÖSD A1 question style.

What's different from generic German apps:

- **Mini-context fill-in-the-blank** — the question gives you a sentence, you fill in the right form. The same shape used by the real exam.
- **Calibrated A1 distractors** — wrong gender on -ung nouns, wrong case after fixed prepositions, wrong perfect-tense auxiliary. The mistakes real A1 candidates make on test day.
- **Per-rule weakness analytics** — find out it's Akkusativ-with-direkte-Objekte, not "German grammar in general", that needs another study session.
- **Vocabulary range stays A1** — no B-level vocabulary creeps into the practice. The level matches your study.
