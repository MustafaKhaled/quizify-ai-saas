**German Grammar A2** ships alongside A1 with full coverage of every A2 grammar rule:

- **Dativ** with all the indirect-object verbs (helfen, danken, gehören, gefallen)
- **Wechselpräpositionen** — when does *in* take Akkusativ vs Dativ, *auf* the table or onto the table
- **Konjunktiv II for politeness** — *möchte*, *könnte*, *hätte gern*
- **Trennbare und untrennbare Verben** in present + Perfekt (the *ge-* placement trap)
- **Nebensätze** with *weil*, *dass*, *wenn*, *als*, *obwohl* — verb-final word order drilled until it's a reflex
- **Plusquamperfekt** for sequencing past events

Distractors are tuned to A2-specific mistakes: wrong case after Wechselpräposition, wrong verb position in subordinate clauses, wrong auxiliary on *sein* / *haben* perfect-tense forms.
