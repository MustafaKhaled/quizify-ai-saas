The first Hören (listening) module is live and it's a step-change for German exam prep.

**What it does**

Click "generate" and ~2 minutes later you have a full Goethe-Zertifikat B1 Hören mock — all four Teile, in the official format, with native German voices:

- **Teil 1 — Kurze Texte:** 5 short single-speaker monologues (announcements, voicemails, weather, traffic), 5 MCQ
- **Teil 2 — Vortrag:** one longer presentation (city tour, museum guide, workshop intro), 5 MCQ
- **Teil 3 — Gespräch:** two-speaker dialogue (friends, colleagues, service interactions), 7 richtig/falsch items
- **Teil 4 — Diskussion:** three-speaker panel (moderator + two guests), 8 "wer sagt was" MCQs

**Why this matters**

Most B1 candidates have read every grammar rule but never sat through a full Hören mock at exam pace. Existing online "B1 Hören practice" reuses the same five recordings until the answers are memorized — not the skill. Quizify generates fresh scripts every time, with distinct native German Azure Neural voices for multi-speaker dialogues so you actually practice tracking who is saying what.

Per-Teil play limits enforced (Teil 4 plays twice; the others play once). After the mock, every question shows the German phrase from the audio that supports the correct answer.

Pro plans include 5 full mocks per rolling 7 days.

**Performance**

All four Teile are generated in parallel — Gemini writes the scripts, Edge TTS renders the audio per Teil concurrently. End-to-end ~2 minutes, comfortably inside our reverse-proxy timeout.
