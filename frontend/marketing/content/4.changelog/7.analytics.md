When you sign up for Quizify, the dashboard now starts with **just the tracks you actually plan to study** — not all eight at once.

**Onboarding (first-time users)**
- Multi-select screen to pick the certifications and language tracks you're prepping for
- Provisions a Subject row per pick so your dashboard, your weak-topic list, and your quiz history are all scoped to your real prep

**Browse Library (returning users)**
- Modal accessible from the dashboard with every available track
- One click to add a track to your library — instant access, no checkout, no friction (Pro required for paid tracks)
- The dashboard's recent-subjects view stays focused on your tracks, not a generic catalog

This came out of feedback that a 9-track catalog on the empty dashboard was overwhelming. Now the dashboard reflects your study plan, and the catalog is one click away when you want to expand it.
