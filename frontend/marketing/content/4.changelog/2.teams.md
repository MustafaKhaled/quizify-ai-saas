Two foundational account features shipped in April:

- **Email verification on signup** — every new account confirms ownership of the address before it can take quizzes, with a verification link that expires in 24 hours
- **Password reset over email** — single-use token, 1-hour expiry, all refresh tokens revoked on use so a forgotten password can't leave a stale session behind

These are unglamorous but load-bearing. Your account is now the durable thing — your trial, your subscription, your quiz history, your weakness analytics — and email verification is what makes that account trustworthy. Account-recovery and onboarding flows we ship from here all build on this.
