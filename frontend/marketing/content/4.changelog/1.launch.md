Quizify AI is live. The first track is **PMP** — the Project Management Professional certification from PMI — built around real outcomes: passing the exam and finding the topics you don't actually know yet.

What's in the PMP track at launch:

- Full coverage of every PMI process group and knowledge area
- Scenario-based MCQs that test the PMI mindset (the heroic-action trap, the customer-collaboration trap, the change-management trap)
- Calibrated distractors that mirror real candidate mistakes
- Per-chapter weakness analytics — the dashboard tells you whether your weak spot is Risk, Procurement, or Stakeholder Engagement
- Real-exam timer for full pacing simulation

The thesis is simple: generic AI quiz tools generate plausible-looking questions; we generate questions that train you for the exam you're actually sitting. PMP is the proof of concept. More tracks coming.
