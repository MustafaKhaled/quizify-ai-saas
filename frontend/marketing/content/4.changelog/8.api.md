Three things we shipped this week to make Quizify's email infrastructure feel like a real product:

**1. Welcome email**
Triggered after Google OAuth first signup OR after successful email verification. Concrete first action ("pick your first track"), a quick rundown of trial allowance, and a real reply-to so the email looks like it came from a person.

**2. Deliverability hardening**
Sender moved from a no-reply alias to `hello@quizifyai.app` — a real, monitored mailbox. Mailbox providers (Gmail, Outlook) weight sender reputation heavily on whether the from-address can actually receive mail and shows interaction. Plus footer contact info to keep the report-spam rate low when users have a question.

**3. One-click unsubscribe**
A new `/unsubscribe` page on the marketing site, plus the `List-Unsubscribe` header on every email pointing at a backend POST endpoint that satisfies RFC 8058 One-Click. Token-authenticated JWT (1-year expiry, type-tagged so it can't be misused as an access token), idempotent — and resubscribe is one click away on the same confirmation page if you change your mind.

Transactional email — verification, password reset, welcome — still sends unconditionally; the unsubscribe flag only suppresses non-essential mail (the foundation for future digest/announcement features).
