## Why Timed Practice is Your Secret Weapon

In the real world, knowledge is only half the battle. The other half is **performance under pressure**. Many students know the material but "freeze up" or run out of time during the actual exam. This is why Quizify AI integrates a customizable Exam Timer into every quiz.

When you practice with a timer, you develop "Exam Stamina." You learn how to manage your 60 seconds per question, how to spot "distractor" answers quickly, and how to maintain focus when the clock is ticking. You are not just studying; you are training like an athlete.
